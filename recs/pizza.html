Pizza

Description

The ingredients required include yeast, sugar, bread flour, olive oil, salt, and toppings of your choice. You need measuring instruments, an oven, and kitchenware in order to complete the recipe. The pizza dough will last about 5 days in the fridge. Store it in airtight storage container for the best preservability. Ensure you have the time to cook the pizza, as it takes slight effort from the user. Ready up and enjoy your pizza!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 0.25 ounce active dry yeast
  • 2.5 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F and lightly grease a pizza pan.
  2. Place warm water in a bowl and add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand for about 10 minutes until a creamy texture appears.
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture. Beat until smooth. Can be done by hand or use a stand mixer with a dough hook.
  4. Let it rest for 5 minutes after dough forms.
  5. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.
  6. Move to the pizza pan.
  7. Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice. This includes cheese, pepperoni slices, olives, spinach, ham, mushrooms, etc.
  8. Bake in preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes preferred. Remove from oven, let it cool for 5 minutes, and enjoy your homemade pizza!
